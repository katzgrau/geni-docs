Get Your GENI Credentials
=========================

Before you can jump into using GENI tools and services, you will need to obtain an account with a GENI clearinghouse.

Mozilla offers GENI access to developers and experimenters participating in the `Mozilla Ignite App Challenge <https://mozillaignite.org/>`_. If you're interested in hopping on GENI and getting access to gigabit speed and OpenFlow testbeds, send a note to `kenny@mozillafoundation.org <mailto:kenny@mozillafoundation.org>`_.
